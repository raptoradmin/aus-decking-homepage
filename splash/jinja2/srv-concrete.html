Acousti-Mat® I

Acousti-Mat® 3 is the sound deadening solution for floor systems that have been impossible to control! Now you can put an end to those “noise” complaints – even with open beam, concrete slab and conventional wood frame systems.
Acousti‑Mat 3 consists of fused, entangled filaments attached to a non-woven fabric, similar to Maxxon’s Acousti‑Mat II, only three times thicker. It’s a proven fact that the larger the air space, the greater the sound control – and this system creates the largest air void of any sound control mat. Acousti-Mat 3 increases IIC levels up to 17 points over wood frame, and up to 25 rating points (or more) over concrete. It also increases the STC rating 6-15 points over a bare wood frame system.
